KV Dharamgarh Heat-Wave Circular

Issuer: Dist. Admin Date: 24 May 2024 Ref: KD/24/881
Jargon Deadline Action
touch_app Click any section below to analyze

In view of the prevailing severe heatwave conditions across the district, it has been decided by the competent authority to implement immediate preventive measures to ensure the safety and well-being of all students and staff members in educational institutions.

All government, government-aided, and private recognized schools under the jurisdiction of KV Dharamgarh shall strictly adhere to the revised operational protocols until further notice.

Outdoor activities, including morning assemblies, physical education classes, and sports events, are strictly prohibited between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM. Indoor alternatives must be arranged where necessary.

schedule TIME-SENSITIVE Para 4

Regarding transportation, the depot management authorities must ensure that all school buses adjust their schedules. Final drop-offs must be completed no later than 11:30 AM. Principals are directed to submit the compliance roster to the district office.

Adequate provisioning of clean drinking water and ORS packets must be made available in all classrooms. Health officers will conduct random inspections starting next Monday.

Any institution found violating these directives will face strict disciplinary action under the Disaster Management Act, 2005.

This order comes into immediate effect and supersedes all previous communications regarding school timings for the summer session.
